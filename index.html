About Me

I want to give you a bit of background about me. I was born on July 5, 2000 and raised in Pasadena, California where I live with my parents as well as my younger sister. My parents immigrated to the United States from Armenia with their brothers, sisters, and cousins and started life anew here. Most of my family now resides in California, making me and my cousins all first-generation Armenian Americans here in the US. Within my family, I was also a first-generation college student, graduating from UCSD in 2024 with a B.S. in Computer Engineering.
