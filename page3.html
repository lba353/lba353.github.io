This is a project I made in 11th grade in my AP United States History class. For our final project, we were tasked with creating anything relating to what we learned in U.S. History. The project could range from making a movie, poster board, scrapbook, or a simple essay. Our team was a group of four; which consisted of Charlie Antoun, Ricardo Harris, Nathanael Chow, and I. The topic we decided on was to make a video on President McKinley's Assassination back in 1901, with the moive taking place in the eyes of his assassin, Leon Czolgosz. What made the project especially interesting was how one of our team members had experience in acting, so he was essentially a director on this project when filming, while the rest of us had little / no experience in acting. The whole project was fun to film and to see it come together was an absolute joy for me, while having some laughs and bloopers along the way.
